FAQ

1. How much does membership cost?

Prices vary based on the type of membership or class pack you choose. We offer flexible options like monthly plans, drop-in rates, and discounted bundles. Reach out or check our website for the most up-to-date pricing.

2. Do I need any experience to join group classes?

Not at all! Our instructors provide modifications so beginners and experienced members alike can participate comfortably.

3. What should I bring to my workout session?

Wear comfortable, athletic clothing and supportive shoes if needed (e.g., for kickboxing). A water bottle and a towel are also recommended for staying hydrated and cleaning up sweat.

4. Can I try a class before committing?

Yes! We offer introductory deals and sometimes free trial classes so you can experience the vibe and see if we're the right fit for your goals.

5. How often can I attend classes each week?

As often as you'd like! We have a flexible schedule with multiple sessions daily. With a membership or class pack, you can pick and choose the classes that work best for your routine.

6. Is there a cancellation or pause policy for memberships?

Yes! If you're traveling, sick, or unable to attend for a set period, you can request to pause or cancel your membership. Just reach out to our team so we can help with the details.

7. Do you offer any nutrition guidance?

We do! While we're not registered dietitians, we provide general nutritional advice and can recommend eating habits to complement your workout routine. We can also refer you to trusted specialists if you need more in-depth guidance.
